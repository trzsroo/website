About Me

My name is Gia, or G, whichever you prefer. I have spent my entire life living in Hubbardston, MA (yes, it does exist; google it). I am of Asian and American descent, which causes some people to stare at both my sister and I. I ended up writing my college essay on the experiences of being a mixed child growing up.

Despite going to a math and science school for both my last two years of high school and college, I enjoy the arts (it’s like I’m a Renaissance [wo]man). I enjoy singing, acting, painting, and photography. I am obsessed with makeup, fashion, and hair.

Feel free to explore my website!

I want this whole thing to be a positive experience for everyone. Please contact me through any of my social media or email me if you have questions, concerns, or any sort of comment. This is a judgement free zone and I want it to be a safe place for everyone.
